We invite you to proudly be a part of science!

  • CitSci India is a platform aimed at introducing Citizen Science to you, a modern and democratic way of doing research. Here you can learn about different projects, use tools, attend training and consume materials.. We hope this will enlighten the scientist within you!

What is citizen science?

  • Citizen science is the collection and analysis of data relating to the natural world by members of the general public, in collaboration with professional scientists. People can contribute to scientific research of various disciplines such as astronomy, natural history, biodiversity, etc. Check out the Tools page to learn how to participate.
